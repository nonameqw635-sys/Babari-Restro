About Baabari Restro & Celebration Hall

Established in 2015, Baabari Restro has become a symbol of luxury, cuisine, and celebration in the heart of Lalitpur. Designed for both fine dining and grand events, our venue blends modern elegance with traditional Nepali hospitality.

With a capacity of over 500 guests, we specialize in weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and social gatherings, offering full-service catering, décor, and world-class entertainment systems.

Our award-winning chefs bring together the best flavors of Nepali, Indian, Chinese, and Continental cuisine — all crafted with fresh, local ingredients.
